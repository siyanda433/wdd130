Our Purpose, Mission & Motto

Purpose: To provide safe, exhilarating whitewater rafting experiences that connect people to the natural beauty of the river while fostering a deep appreciation for the environment and promoting responsible outdoor adventure. Mission: Aims to provide life-changing travel experiences that have a positive impact on their guests, guides, and the places they visit. They also strive to respect Indigenous peoples and the Earth. Motto: Ride the Rapids of Adventure! Paddle Hard, Play Harder Flow with the Water, Feel the Thrill Adventure Awaits Downstream Row, Row, Row Your Boat, Gently Down the Stream Whitewater Wonderland Awaits! Get Wet, Go Wild!

History

Back Then.

Founded in 1995, White Water Rafting started as a small family-owned business in the heart of Colorado's Rocky Mountains. With a passion for adventure and a commitment to safety, the company quickly became a favorite among thrill-seekers. Over the years, it expanded its offerings to include guided tours on some of the most challenging rivers in the United States. Known for its expert guides and eco-friendly practices, White Water Rafting has earned a reputation for providing unforgettable experiences while promoting environmental conservation. Today, it remains a leader in the industry, attracting adventurers from around the world.

Currently

Today, White Water Rafting continues to innovate in the adventure tourism industry. The company has expanded its environmental initiatives, implementing sustainable practices and supporting local conservation efforts along Colorado's waterways. Their expert guides now lead expeditions on Class II-V rapids throughout the Arkansas River Basin, including the famous Royal Gorge section [source] (https://www.coolworks.com/royal-gorge-zip-line-tours/profile). The company has also embraced modern safety technologies while maintaining their commitment to personalized service. Their dedication to both thrilling adventures and environmental stewardship has earned them numerous industry accolades and a loyal international client base.
