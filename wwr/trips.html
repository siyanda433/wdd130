Aussenkehr Adventure

Experience a family friendly, guided rafting adventure with two nights at base camp and three nights under the stars on the banks of the mighty Orange River. This adventure boasts beautiful, pristine scenery, wildlife, bird- and fish species and flows between the spectacular Richtersveld Mountains from Vioolsdrift to Aussenkehr for approximately 65km.

  • Distance: 65km
  • Difficulty: hard
  • Duration: 4-5 days

ADVENTURE RAFTING & CANOEING

Rafting & Canoeing adventures on the Orange River are undoubtedly one of the most popular tours. Bundi Adventurers is a tour operator that offers various tours of river rafting and canoe excursions on the Richtersveld section. We operate from our base camp located on the banks of the Orange River. It is approximately 10km from the Namibia border at Noordoewer – on the Namibian side. Rafting and Canoeing are fun and healthy sports to participate in. Rafting is an outdoor sport in which participants use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. Canoeing is the sport of paddling a light, narrow boat.

  • Distance: 10km
  • Difficulty: medium
  • Duration: 2 days

White water - Tugela

After summer rains, the still pools of the Tugela River turn into an exciting stretch of white water. From our bush camp, nestled in the heart of the Tugela Canyon, there is a 20km stretch of rapids and pools on which the SA white water rafting champs have been hosted. Water levels rise and fall, with 8 and 4-man rafts used in higher volumes whilst the two man crocs and kayaks are ideal for lower levels.

  • Distance: 20km
  • Difficulty: easy
  • Duration: 1-2 days
